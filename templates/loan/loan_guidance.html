BCS Loan Policy & Guidelines

Before applying for a loan, please read the following guidelines carefully:

Types of Loans

  • Qard Hasan (Cash Loan): Interest-free loans given on the basis of trust.
  • Murabaha (Asset-Based Financing): Loans provided for purchasing assets.

Requirements to Apply

  • You must be an active employee of the organization.
  • You need two guarantors for loan approval.
  • Repayment must be made within the agreed period.
