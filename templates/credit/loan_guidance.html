Shariah-Compliant Credit Options

Benevolence Cooperative Society (BCS) provides Shariah-compliant credit facilities that align with Islamic financial principles, ensuring members can access financial support without engaging in interest-based transactions. The available credit options include:

1. Qard Hasan (Cash Credit Financing)

Qard Hasan is a benevolent cash credit facility where members receive financial assistance with the understanding that the amount borrowed will be repaid without interest.

  • Members apply for cash(Qard Hasan) credit.
  • BCS disburses funds based on eligibility.
  • Repayment is made within an agreed period.
  • A fixed administrative charge may apply.

2. Murabaha (Asset-Based Financing)

Murabaha is an asset-based financing model where BCS facilitates the purchase of goods or assets on behalf of a member and sells them at an agreed markup price.

  • A member requests financing for an asset (e.g., equipment, vehicles, or business inventory).
  • BCS purchases the asset from a supplier.
  • BCS sells the asset to the member at a pre-agreed profit margin.
  • The member repays in installments over a defined period
  • A fixed administrative charge may apply.

3. Musharakah (Partnership Financing)

Musharakah is a joint investment model where both BCS and the member contribute capital towards a business venture or asset acquisition. Profits are shared based on an agreed ratio, while losses are distributed according to the capital contribution of each party.

  • The member and BCS co-invest in a business or asset.
  • Profits are shared based on a pre-agreed ratio.
  • Losses are shared according to capital contribution.
  • Ownership gradually transfers to the member over time.

4. Ijarah (Lease Financing)

Ijarah is a leasing (rent) arrangement where BCS purchases an asset and leases it to a member.

  • BCS purchases the asset (e.g., vehicle, equipment, real estate).
  • Member leases (rents) the asset and makes regular payments.
  • Ownership transfer may be possible at the end of the lease period.
